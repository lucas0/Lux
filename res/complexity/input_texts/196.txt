b'The A.C.L.U.s brief said officials had not responded adequately to the crisis in the prison.\n\nThe government has demonstrated for months that it will not take the necessary action at Elkton to save the lives of its charges, the groups brief said. As of today, despite the lack of mass testing, there are 128 confirmed and currently active cases of Covid-19 among Elkton prisoners, plus eight more among staff. The true number is likely far higher. Yet not a single prisoner, no matter how vulnerable, has been transferred out.'

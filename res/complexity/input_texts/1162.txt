b'What to watch: Tennessee Republicans are wrapping up a bitter, expensive campaign for governor, with four leading contenders attacking one another as insufficiently loyal to President Trump. Those candidates are Representative Diane Black; Beth Harwell, the speaker of the Tennessee House of Representatives; Randy Boyd, a former state economic development commissioner; and Bill Lee, a businessman. The Democratic primary is far quieter, with former Mayor Karl Dean of Nashville heavily favored.

Whats going on?\n\nAfter a week in which international travel was largely shut down, restrictions on domestic travel have begun. Hawaii and Florida both sought to cut down on the number of out-of-state travelers by announcing that some newly arriving visitors would have to self-quarantine for 14 days. In Florida, the quarantine only applies to those who fly to the state from New York or New Jersey.'
